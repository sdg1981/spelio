# Animals 1

## Status

Approved editorial draft.

This document represents the current approved editorial version of Animals 1.

It may continue to evolve through learner and teacher feedback, but it is no longer considered a temporary draft.

## Purpose

Introduce highly recognisable Welsh animal vocabulary with low spelling load for learners who have completed Welsh Spelling Foundations.

The goal is to build confidence rather than maximise vocabulary coverage.

## Editorial Principles

* Accessible spellings.
* Highly expected animal vocabulary.
* No deliberate stretch words.
* Carefully curated rather than comprehensive.
* Approximately 10 words.

## Editorial Notes

This collection established several editorial principles for future Topic Collections:

* Topic expectation is more important than perfect category balance.
* Representative Role supports editorial judgement but must not dominate selection.
* Topic 1 should not contain deliberate stretch words.
* Not every Core vocabulary candidate must appear in Topic 1-3.
* Quality of the collection is more important than vocabulary coverage.
